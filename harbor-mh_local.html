Welcome to Harbor’s Edge,

A Hampton Roads full-service Continuing Care Retirement Community.
We offer residents the security of a True LifeCare Program which provides a full continuum of care and valuable estate protection through the use of entrance fee options. The refundable entry deposit plan guarantees our residents’ financial independence and protects their estate for the future. Our new expansion, River Tower at Harbor’s Edge, is under development, but you can start planning and dreaming about it today. We invite you to learn more about securing your place in River Tower at Harbor’s Edge.

Experience Norfolk

While theres always something fun to do here at Harbors Edge, there is an even bigger world of things to do just steps away. Literally. From our front door, you can walk a couple blocks north to Ghent and the eclectic Colley Avenue district. A few blocks the other way puts you in Downtown Norfolk, the regions arts, entertainment, shopping and dining heart and soul. As one of our residents puts it, We have a wonderful community here, but were also connected to the larger community of the city and Downtown.
